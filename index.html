About The Event

イベントについて

Our Vision

百人一首をテーマに

Global Green Event was founded in 2022 with a mission to accelerate the transition to sustainable living through innovation, collaboration, and education. This annual gathering brings together thought leaders, entrepreneurs, designers, and citizens committed to creating a more sustainable world.

百人一首をテーマにしたイベントです。百人一首は偉人が詠んだうたをカルタにしたものです。うんたらかんたら。

How to Participate

参加方法

Registration is open to everyone passionate about sustainability. Join us as an attendee, speaker, exhibitor, or volunteer. The event features workshops, panel discussions, exhibitions, and networking opportunities across multiple cities worldwide.

難しい登録などはありません。ガチャを回して、出た場所に行くだけ。なんかもらえると思います。

About Us

私たちについて

Our Mission

私たちのミッション

We are a global collective of environmentalists, innovators, and changemakers dedicated to accelerating the transition to a sustainable future. Through our annual Global Green Event, we bring together diverse perspectives and expertise to tackle the world's most pressing environmental challenges.

私たちは、京都の観光業を流動化させるために活動しています。

Our Values

私たちの価値観

  • Innovation that respects planetary boundaries
  • 京都を盛り上げる
  • Inclusivity and global collaboration
  • 学生主導
  • Actionable solutions for real-world impact
  • 非営利
  • Knowledge sharing and open-source approaches
  • とかなんとか。
